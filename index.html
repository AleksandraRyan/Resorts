14 Best Beach Resorts in the USA

Written by Aleksandra Ryan
February 28, 2021

With more than 12,000 miles of coastline in the mainland US, America is home to some wonderful beach resorts that make for perfect vacation getaways. From luxury properties designed with romance in mind to kid-friendly resorts on family-friendly beaches, there are resorts for all budgets and styles of traveler.

This includes spots like Hawaii's famous Waikiki Beach on the island of Oahu, which is popular with water sports enthusiasts looking to try their hand at surfing, and Hilton Head or Myrtle Beach in South Carolina, a state known for its pretty beaches and hospitality.

From trendy Miami Beach in Florida and Laguna Beach just south of Los Angeles to the natural beauty of Cannon Beach in Oregon, check out our list of the best beach resorts in the USA.

1. The Setai, Miami Beach, FL

With direct access to the trendiest stretch of Miami Beach, South Beach, The Setai is a stunning property. The Asian-inspired resort is a top choice for couples thanks to its spacious suites that range in size from studios to a stunning four-bedroom penthouse. All feature high-end perks like hand-crafted Duxiana beds covered in high-end Italian sheets, espresso machines, and rainfall showers.

Amenities here include three swimming pools and access to sun-loungers on a private stretch of beach. There is also free yoga on this beach for hotel guests. Additionally there is a super posh spa and a state-of-the-art fitness center on-site.

For dining, choose from Jaya, which serves Asian fare, and The Ocean Grill, which does an international menu including wood-fired seafood items.

Address: 2001 Collins Ave, Miami Beach, Florida

Accommodation: The Setai, Miami Beach

2. Wild Dunes Resort, Isle of Palms, SC

Just across the Intracoastal Waterway (linked by a bridge) from Charleston, Isle of Palms is a beautiful barrier island known for its six miles of white sandy beaches fronting the Atlantic Ocean. Here, you'll find the Wild Dunes Resort, which features two hotels, as well as beach house and condo rentals (the largest has seven bedrooms). As such, the resort does a great job of catering to everyone from couples to multi-generational families.

The Village at Wild Dunes Hotel is one of the properties. It is family friendly and has large, modern suites.

The Boardwalk Inn is the other on-site hotel. It has an adult-only swimming pool.

There are a number of restaurant choices on the grounds, as well as a spa and two 18-hole golf courses.

Address: 200 Grand Pavilion Blvd, Isle of Palms, South Carolina

Accommodation: Wild Dunes Resort

3. Montage Laguna Beach, CA

Book one of the beachfront bungalows or villas at the 5-star Montage Laguna Beach. Located on a bluff fronting the sea just 2.5 miles from the attractions of downtown Laguna Beach, the craftsman-style resort features elegant rooms and suites done up with gorgeous dark wooden furniture and cool regional artwork. All come with private balconies and ocean views along with swanky marble baths.

Amenities here include three swimming pools, including one that faces the Pacific Ocean and has cabana rentals. There is also a restaurant and grill on-site, plus a relatively large spa and assorted shops, including an art gallery.

Address: 30801 South Coast Highway, Laguna Beach, California

Accommodation: Montage Laguna Beach
